Meet the Founder

Katarina Raskovic

Founder of Asset District. Business Development & Growth Consultant. Based in Dubai — working globally.

Before I ever wrote a strategy deck, I spent six years inside the world of motorsport — coordinating national and international events with the Moto Association of Serbia, managing partnerships, logistics, and operations at a level where there is no room for improvisation. Motorsport teaches you something most business schools never do: that precision is not optional, discipline is not a personality trait, and the margin between winning and losing is almost always in the preparation.

That mindset never left me. It became the way I work.

I've since built brands, launched digital platforms — including Načisto, a marketplace connecting businesses with operational professionals — and consulted founders across industries who were ready to stop guessing and start building with intention. My clients don't come to me for generic advice. They come because they want someone who will sit in the detail with them, challenge what isn't working, and build something that holds.

I believe great businesses are built the same way great races are won — with structure, clarity, and an absolute refusal to leave results to chance.
